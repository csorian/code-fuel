# Cheese, Ham and Broccoli Pasta

* Prep: 10 minutes
* Cook: 20 minutes
* Serves: 4

## Ingredients

* 300g pasta, farfella or conchiglie
* 1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
* 1 tbsp oil
* 1 onion, finely chopped
* 2 garlic cloves, crushed
* 250g ham, cut into chunk
* 300ml double cream
* 1 tbsp English mustard
* 140g mature cheddar, grated

## Method

1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions.
2. Add broccoli florets for last 4 mins.
3. Drain and set aside.
4. Heat oil in a large pan.
5. Cook onion for 5 mins.
6. Stir in garlic, cook for 1 min. 
7. Add ham, cream and mustard.
8. Bring to the boil.
9. Add pasta and broccoli.
10. Stir in the cheese.
